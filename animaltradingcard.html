Komachiyo

Komachiyo is the name or a specific Koma Inu found in the land of Wano in the anime One Piece.

      Species: Koma Inu
      Diet: Carnivorous
      Origin: Japan
      Size varies: small dog to large lion

Komainu's are holy animals that are sacred to shrines in Japan. They look like a combination of a dog and a lion with a pig snout, a large curly mane and sharp teeth.
